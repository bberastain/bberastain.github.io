Price

How Price is Determined

Since I began working as a tutor in 2015, I've gained experience by individually tutoring over 30 students. I've spent a lot of time thinking about the best way to teach, and received a lot of feedback on my teaching style. I now focus on both teaching the course material and improving study habits that carry over to other courses. Travel to your home is included in my hourly rate, and all students who maintain a weekly session are welcome to email or text me with additional questions when they need help between tutoring sessions.

Hourly Rate

One-on-one session
$60 per hour
Group session (2 or more)
$40 per hour (for each student)

Acceptable payment methods include cash, check, and Venmo.
