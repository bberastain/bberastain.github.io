Experience

Education

In 2015 I graduated from UC Santa Barbara with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, a Minor in Science and Mathematics Education, and a Minor in Music. Prior to that I attended California High School in San Ramon. I also speak fluent Spanish.

Work with Students

While at UCSB I spent a quarter as a teacher-aid for a bilingual high school math class, helping both English and Spanish speaking students. After college I spent the 2016 and 2017 school years as a substitute teacher for the San Ramon Valley Unified School District, where I worked with elementary, middle, and high schoolers across the district. Being a substitute teacher also gave me the opportunity to attend Staff Developent days and participate in workshops for math educators. In the evenings I tutored over twenty middle school and high school students in Pre-Algebra, Algebra, Geometry, and Trigonometry/Pre-Calculus. I've also taught music to elementary and middle school students, specifically rap and digital audio production, and I've taught all ages to use radio equipment as a DJ for Santa Barbara's radio station KCSB.
